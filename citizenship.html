What makes a good citizen?

Honesty

Honesty is a key element of a good citizen. This is because honesty fosters communication and co-operation between citizens within a society.

Compassion

Compassion is a key element of being a good citizen because citizens in a community have the responsibility of aiding others in the community and compassion compels this action.

Respect

Respect is necessary for being a good citizen because it is essential to respect authority and and others around you.
